# Fact base — Rules of the road

Sources: National Road Traffic Act 93/1996 + Regulations; [SA Learner Driver Manual — Rules of the Road](https://drivecodrivingschool.co.za/assets/files/1_Rules_of_the_Road_draft1.pdf); [Arrive Alive](https://www.arrivealive.mobi/drunk-driving-and-road-safety); [Drive South Africa](https://www.drivesouthafrica.com/en/blog/drunk-driving-in-south-africa-laws).

## Hard numbers (each = 1+ question)
- Speed defaults: 60 km/h urban · 100 km/h other public roads · 120 km/h freeway.
- Blood alcohol: < 0,05 g/100 ml (professional < 0,02); breath < 0,24 mg/1000 ml.
- Ages: learner's (Code 8) 17; full licence 18; learner must be supervised by licensed driver in the vehicle.
- Following distance: 2 s dry, 4 s wet (K53 defensive-driving standard).
- Parking: within 450 mm of kerb; not within 5 m of an intersection; left side of two-way roads only.
- Towing by rope: max 30 km/h; connecting length max 3,5 m; person steering towed vehicle must be licensed.
- Rear load projection: max 1,8 m; red flag by day / red light at night.
- Crash duties: stop, assist, exchange details, report to police within 24 h.
- Hooter: only to warn of danger; not in urban areas otherwise.

## AARTO (from facts/aarto.md)
- Learner suspension threshold: > 6 points; licensed: > 15. 1 point expires per 3 clean months; 3-month suspension per point over.
