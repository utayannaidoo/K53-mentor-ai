# Fact base — AARTO demerit system

Sources: [Moneyweb (1 Sep 2026 demerit start)](https://www.moneyweb.co.za/news/south-africa/driving-licence-demerit-point-system-set-to-start-on-1-september-2026/); [Daily Maverick explainer](https://www.dailymaverick.co.za/article/2026-06-29-new-demerit-system-what-south-african-motorists-need-to-know/); [aboutaarto.co.za/demerits](https://www.aboutaarto.co.za/demerits).

## Verified facts (as of July 2026 — re-verify before each batch)
- Rollout: phase 2 national rollout from 1 Dec 2025 (69 municipalities); phase 3 remaining 144 municipalities 1 Apr 2026; **demerit points allocated from 1 Sep 2026**.
- Thresholds: learner driver suspension when exceeding **6 points**; licensed driver **15 points**.
- Each point over the threshold = **3-month suspension**.
- Expiry: 1 point removed per 3 months with no new points.
- Rehabilitation programme: −4 points, usable once per 12 months.
- Two suspensions then exceeding again → licence cancellation (verify exact clause before publishing).

## Example point values (illustrative — re-verify against the published AARTO schedule)
- Failing to stop at a stop street / stop line: **2 points** (plus fine).
- Driving without a valid licence: **6 points** (plus fine).
- Points are added when the fine is **paid**, an **enforcement order** is issued, or on **conviction** — paying the fine does not stop the points being recorded.
- The schedule lists 2 600+ infringements, each with a fine and point value scaled to severity.
- Confirmed again July 2026: [aarto.co.za — points demerit system](https://www.aarto.co.za/understanding-aarto/points-demerit-system/); [The Citizen — AARTO explained](https://www.citizen.co.za/network-news/motoring/2025/10/08/aarto-explained-fines-demerit-points-and-what-it-means-for-south-african-drivers/).
